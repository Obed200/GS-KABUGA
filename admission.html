About Our Admissions Process

At G.S SAINT ANTOINE , we pride ourselves on offering a straightforward and inclusive admissions process designed to welcome students from all backgrounds. Our goal is to help every student achieve their full potential in a nurturing academic environment.

Steps to Apply:

  1. Fill out the online application form.
  2. Submit the required documents, including previous academic records.
  3. Attend an interview or placement test, if applicable.
  4. Receive your admission letter and begin your journey at G.S SAINT ANTOINE .

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us.
